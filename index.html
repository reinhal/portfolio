About Me

The problem solving and creative thinking required in software development appeals to my spirit as an educator. I embrace hard work. I believe in life-long learning. Inspired by useful technology making a difference in the world, I stepped into the world of software engineering. For several years I have built my skills while walking deeper into programming. I have built apps based on real needs and a belief that I could find ways to help people. As my skills improved and interest deepened, I knew I had discovered my calling.

When I am not in front of my computer, I enjoy tending to my bees, reading, traveling and doing whatever I can outside. I also manage the development of an application I designed for Montessori teachers, Montessori Notes.

The projects listed below are my work. I am responsible for every aspect of each one. Look at my resume to learn about my professional experience.

JusKidin is an all-in-one web tool for people who have active kids they love! Life with kids is amazing, but managing and tracking it all MUST be less tedious and MUST become more fun. With JusKidin the user can create individual profiles for each child by simply entering a name and a birthdate. The profile gives access to this child's relevant developmental web resources. Drawers can be created and used to organize images by topic (schoolwork, artwork, cards created), events, activities or some other custom title.

JusNoshin allows users to find recipes by selecting preferred dietary requirements, cuisines and allergies/intolerances. After selecting a recipe from the suggested results, the user is given an appropriate wine pairing for that particular recipe. My family has a lot of dietary preferences and restrictions. We wanted a tool to help find good recipes we could prepare together, and for the adults, a tasty wine.
