About Me

Problem solving and creative thinking, required in software development, appeals to my spirit as an educator. I embrace hard work. I believe in life-long learning. Inspired by useful technology making a difference in the world, I stepped into the world of software engineering. For several years I have built my skills while walking deeper into programming. I have built apps based on real needs and a belief that I could find ways to help people. As my skills improved and interest deepened, I knew I had discovered my calling.

When I am not in front of my computer, I enjoy tending to my bees, reading, traveling and doing whatever I can outside. I also manage the development of an application I designed for Montessori teachers, Montessori Notes.

JusKidin is an all-in-one web tool for parents. The user creates profiles for each child by entering a name and a birthdate. Then, through a custom Google API, parents can get access to child developmental information for each child. Additionally, the user can create drawers. These drawers can be used to organize images by topic, event or some other custom title. Parents have a lot to manage and track. The vision of this app is to give parents a real tool to help. Also, children attend many events, create art and school projects. By digitizing these items and memories, parents will be able to keep them all.
